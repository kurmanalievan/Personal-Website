About me!

I am from a small but beautiful country, Kyrgyzstan. I have a big family, three brothers and two sisters, I am the fourth child. My father is an architect and my mother was a mathematician. Despite any obstacles, my parents would always try to give the best for us. We grew up in a loving and caring family. Four years ago, my mom passed away from cancer. I am a bird with a broken wing.

Programming!

During my high school, I did not have regular access to a computer or internet, so I thought that technology is something that I will never understand. And now [drum sound] I am pursuing my BSc in Computer Science in Budapest, life is unexpected, isn’t it? During my foundation year at American University of Central Asia, I used to attend the programmers club, just to stare at those ‘blue blooded’ people - programmers. That was a turning point. I saw how they create cool animations, games, and websites from nothing, in a few hours! I've understood how much power a computer may hold. So, in summer, after (30*12) hours of standing in the street and selling ice cream, I earned my own money and [drum sound] bought my own laptop, and that’s where it had begun.
